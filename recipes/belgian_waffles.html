Belgian Waffles

Everyone loves a light and fluffy Belgin Waffles

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs, separated
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup oil(vegetable or canola oil)

Steps

  1. sift together flour, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl
  2. Separate the egg yolks and whites into two different mixing bowls
  3. Add the milk and oil to the bowl with yolks. Stir in the dry ingredients
  4. Beat the egg whites with a mixer until stiff peaks form. Then gently fold the egg whites to the batter. Be careful not to overmix.
  5. Spoon batter into preheated waffle iron and cook according to waffle maker instructions.
