TOAST

The BEST and simplest homemade Bread recipe! It's simply heaven.

Ingredients

  • Warm water
  • Active Dry yeast
  • Granulated sugar or honey
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Prepare the dough
  3. Knead the dough
  4. First rise
  5. Punch the dough down
  6. Divide into two equal portions
  7. Second rise
  8. Bake
